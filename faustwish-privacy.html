PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: June 2026

1. WHAT THIS APP IS

Faust Wish is an entertainment application powered by artificial intelligence. It is not a professional service of any kind. All responses are generated by AI for entertainment purposes only.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

Faust Wish does not collect, store, or retain any personal data.

When you speak a wish, your voice is transcribed locally on your device using the device's built-in speech recognition. The resulting text is sent to our AI API for processing. We do not store, log, or retain your wishes or FAUST's responses.

We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Faust Wish uses the following third-party services:

Groq API — processes your transcribed wish to generate a response. Groq's privacy policy applies to data sent to their servers. No wish is stored on our end.

Google AdMob — serves ads to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Google Play Billing — handles in-app purchases. Payment data is managed entirely by Google.

4. IN-APP PURCHASES

The "Eternal Pact" is a one-time in-app purchase that grants unlimited access to our API. Purchase data is handled by Google Play. We do not receive or store payment information.

Responses may be subject to limitations imposed by the external AI provider.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Faust Wish is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are under 13, do not use this app.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
